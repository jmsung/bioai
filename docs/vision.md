# Vision

A multi-agent AI system that integrates diverse biomedical knowledge domains to support **personalized health guidance** and **early intervention**.

Rather than replacing physicians, the goal is to build a structured decision-support system that promotes preventive care and continuous health monitoring.

## Specialized Agents

- **Genomics** — Variant interpretation
- **Transcriptomics** — Gene expression signals
- **Proteomics** — Biomarker inference
- **Pharmacology** — Drug interaction reasoning
- **Clinical Guidelines** — Guideline interpretation
- **Literature Review** — Latest medical publications

## Capabilities

Given a patient's biological data and health profile, the agents collaborate to:

1. Detect early risk signals before disease onset
2. Recommend preventive strategies
3. Suggest lifestyle modifications (diet, exercise)
4. Flag when clinical evaluation or hospital visit is necessary
5. Provide evidence-grounded reasoning with citations
